### 6 August 2025: Paper Accepted at ASRU 2025 Workshop

Our paper, **"Acoustic to Articulatory Speech Inversion for Children with Velopharyngeal Insufficiency,"** has been accepted for presentation at the **Automatic Speech Recognition Understanding (ASRU) Workshop 2025**.  

This work presents advances in **speech inversion systems** applied to clinical assessment and provides insights into estimating articulatory movements in children with velopharyngeal insufficiency.

### 19 May 2025: Three Papers Accepted at Interspeech 2025

I am pleased to announce that **three of our papers** have been accepted for presentation at **Interspeech 2025**:  

1. **FT-Boosted SV: Towards Noise Robust Speaker Verification for English Speaking Classroom Environments**  
2. **Enhancing Acoustic-to-Articulatory Speech Inversion by Incorporating Nasality**  
3. **Subtyping Speech Errors in Childhood Speech Sound Disorders with Acoustic-to-Articulatory Speech Inversion**

These papers reflect ongoing advances in **speech inversion systems**, robust speaker verification, and clinical applications of speech processing for children with speech disorders.
