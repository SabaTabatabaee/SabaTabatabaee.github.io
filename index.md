I'm Saba Tabatabaee. Currently, I am pursuing a Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Maryland - College Park, where I have the privilege of working with [Prof. Carol Espy-Wilson](https://isr.umd.edu/clark/faculty/391/Carol-Espy-Wilson).
My research focuses on speech processing, where I integrate concepts from signal processing, speech science, linguistics, and machine learning. 
I have worked on various speech processing research projects, including robust speaker verification under classroom noise conditions and speech enhancement. 
Currently, my research focuses on developing a speech inversion AI tool that estimates vocal tract variables such as lip and tongue movements and nasal airflow from far-field audio.
