**Three of our papers** have been accepted for presentation at **Interspeech 2025**:  

1. **[FT-Boosted SV: Towards Noise-Robust Speaker Verification for English Speaking Classroom Environments](https://www.isca-archive.org/interspeech_2025/tabatabaee25_interspeech.html)**  
2. **[Enhancing Acoustic-to-Articulatory Speech Inversion by Incorporating Nasality](https://www.isca-archive.org/interspeech_2025/tabatabaee25b_interspeech.html)**  
3. **[Subtyping Speech Errors in Childhood Speech Sound Disorders with Acoustic-to-Articulatory Speech Inversion](https://www.isca-archive.org/interspeech_2025/benway25_interspeech.html)**  

These papers highlight ongoing advances in **speech inversion systems**, robust speaker verification, and clinical applications of speech processing for children with speech disorders.
